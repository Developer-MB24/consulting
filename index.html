Welcome!

Why I Do What I Do

Throughout my career as a Licensed Nursing Home Administrator (LNHA), MBA graduate, and seasoned Healthcare Executive, I’ve worn many hats — and each one has reinforced a deep truth: there’s a powerful need for clear guidance, structured mentorship, and authentic leadership in the healthcare industry.

From hospitals and long-term care facilities to behavioral health centers, I’ve witnessed incredibly talented individuals struggle to align their skills with their career goals — not because they lacked potential, but because they lacked support.

That’s why this platform exists.

I created this space to be your professional compass. Whether you're a nursing student unsure of your next step, a new graduate overwhelmed by options, or a healthcare professional seeking a purposeful pivot — I’m here for you.

Who I Help

This isn’t a one-size-fits-all coaching model. I work specifically with:

• Healthcare students looking to design a career roadmap with clarity

• New graduates exploring clinical or administrative healthcare pathways

• Nurses and practitioners transitioning into leadership roles

• International medical graduates navigating U.S. healthcare systems

• Behavioral health professionals seeking regulatory and operational expertise

• Aspiring administrators pursuing LNHA licensure or healthcare leadership roles

Strategic Career Planning

Real-World Experience

My Story

I started my journey like many others — filled with passion for healthcare and a drive to make a difference. But as I stepped into roles with greater responsibility, I realized something critical: great clinicians don’t always know how to become great leaders.

I’ve led teams. I’ve turned around facilities. I’ve created systems that work — and I’ve helped people rediscover their "why" in a system that often forgets the human side of healthcare.

Now, I want to bring that same transformation to your life and your career.

Because if you’re going to pour your heart into this work — you deserve to do it with direction, fulfillment, and confidence.
